Cube vs Sphere

About

Conspiracy! The Cube Kingdom is left outraged as King Cubulus III grieves for his 457 missing children, all kidnapped one fateful Tuesday afternoon. As we commiserate the king's loss, we can't help but wonder, is there someone out there, some devilishly handsome rogue, who would step forward and vow to bring back King Cubulus' children safe and sound, no matter the cost? Will you rise to the occasion, and bring stability to a kingdom in turmoil? Or will you succumb to the temptations of the evil Spheres? All of this, and more, in Cube vs. Sphere

Development

Cube vs. Sphere is the personal project of the former president of our club, Josh Owens. CvS is a time based mobile game in which you have to quickly navigate procedurally generated caves looking for a cube that you have to collide with to end the level. While doing this you must dodge sphere shaped enemies and collect power ups to either evade these enemies or clear the level faster.
