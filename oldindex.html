About

We are a community based around encouraging game development and the acquisition of game developement skills for students at the University of Maryland College Park. We hold weekly meetings/workshops aimed at fostering these skills and host events and and competitions related to game development.

Education

Help students develop marketable game dev skills by teaching them to be proficent in relevant game dev software and programming languages

Experience

Provide students with opportunities and resources to finish and even publish small scale video games or projects either in groups or on solo projects.

Community

Foster a sense of community and interest surrounding game development.


The club's primary goals are to:

  1. Help students develop marketable game dev skills and experiences. This means helping them becoming proficent in relevant game dev software and programming languages
  2. Provide students with opportunities and resources to finish and even publish small scale video games or projects.
  3. Foster a sense of community and interest surrounding game development.
