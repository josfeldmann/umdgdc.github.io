Home of the UMD Game Developers Club

The Game Developers Club is a student run organisation focused on promoting and encouraging personal and professional Game Development at the University of Maryland College Park. We do this by hosting game dev related events like game jams and workshops, and provide game dev resources for students to use. We host at least one 24+ hour game jam per semester, numerous smaller 2-6 hour "game dashes", and hold weekly meetings, workshops, and labs. If you are interested in joining you are welcome to join our Facebook group and Discord using the links below, and check our Events page to find out when our next meeting is.
